# Play Store listing copy

## Title (≤ 30 chars)

> TrotroOS — Kumasi Trotro

## Short description (≤ 80 chars)

> Live trotro seats, fares and pickup spots across Kumasi.

## Full description (≤ 4 000 chars)

> **Stop guessing if the trotro is coming. See it on the map.**
>
> TrotroOS shows live trotro vehicles, queued passengers, fares and pickup spots across Kumasi — from KNUST, Ayeduase and Bomso to Adum, Suame and Tafo Pankrono.
>
> **For passengers**
> • See nearby mates and which routes they're running, in real time.
> • Reserve a seat before you get to the station.
> • Use *My location* or search any place by name.
> • Track your ride to pickup with live ETA.
> • Pay your mate in cash or MoMo when you board.
>
> **For mates**
> • Set custom origin and destination.
> • See passenger demand live, by route.
> • Ratings and earnings tools as you drive.
>
> **Built for Kumasi**
> • Battery-aware: GPS only while you actively use the app.
> • Works on patchy data: routes and fares cached on first launch.
> • No background location tracking.
>
> Have feedback? Reach us in-app, or at support@trotroos.com.

## What's new (release notes for v1.3.0)
